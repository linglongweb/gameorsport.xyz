About Count Masters

Count Masters is an exciting multiplayer running game where you control a crowd of stickmen. Your goal is to collect more stickmen, avoid obstacles, and reach the finish line before your opponents.

The game combines strategy with quick reflexes as you must decide when to split your group, when to merge, and how to navigate through various obstacles. Each level presents new challenges and opportunities to outsmart your opponents.

Game Controls

Mouse Controls

  • Left Click: Move your stickman group
  • Click and Hold: Split your group

Touch Controls

  • Tap: Move your stickman group
  • Tap and Hold: Split your group
